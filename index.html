The Problem
Two friends. Same Royal Enfield. Same service.
One paid ₹4,000. The other paid ₹2,500.
Neither got a bill that meant anything.

That's not an edge case — that's the everyday reality for crores of two-wheeler owners across India. Your mechanic is skilled, your mechanic is trustworthy, but the tools he works with haven't changed in 30 years. A notebook. A mental ledger. A handshake.

No itemised bill. No service history. No reminder when the next oil change is due. When the customer drives away, there's no record for either side. Trust gets built — and lost — on gut feel alone.

Namma Garage changes that. We give independent mechanics the same digital infrastructure that big brand showrooms have had for years — simple enough to use on a ₹10,000 phone, powerful enough to run an entire garage.

How it works

From first visit to paid invoice
in under 2 minutes

Designed for mechanics, not software engineers.

01

Enter customer details

Type the phone number. If they've visited before, everything auto-fills — name, bike, plate number. New customer? One quick form.

Returns auto-recognised
02

Pick services from your catalog

Tap oil change, brake pad, spark plug — or any custom service you've added. Prices are pre-set but editable per job. Add labour charges separately.

Your catalog, your prices
03

Save and send the invoice

Mark as Paid or Unpaid. Hit send — and the customer gets a proper WhatsApp bill in seconds. You get a permanent record forever.
